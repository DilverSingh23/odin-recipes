Cheese Pizza

Description

This is the best cheese pizza recipe! Homemade Pizza sauce is slathered onto an easy homemade pizza dough, then topped with lots of mozzarella cheese! Learn how to make cheese pizza at home better than your favorite restaurant!

Perfected cheese pizza recipe (using homemade pizza dough). It's loaded with mozzarella cheese making a delicious pie that will become a favorite for years to come.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese

Pizza Dough:

  • 1 cup warm water (105 degrees F)
  • 1 Tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups all purpose-flour
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt

Steps

Make The Pizza Dough:

  1. Combine warm water, yeast and sugar in a large mixing bowl and stir to combine.
  2. Let mixture sit for 5 minutes, or until it becomes frothy and bubbles form.
  3. Gently stir in olive oil.
  4. Add 2 cups of flour and salt and mix with a spatula until a ball begins to form (dough will still be slightly sticky). Add more flour as needed to form a dough ball.
  5. Transfer to a floured surface and knead into a smooth dough, adding up to ½ cup extra flour if needed.

Make The Pizza:

  1. Put a pizza stone in the oven and preheat the oven to 500 degrees F.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough to a 14” circle on a piece of parchment paper (for easy transfer to the pizza stone).
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Use a pizza peel to transfer the pizza on the parchment paper onto the baking stone in the oven.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-14 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven (using the pizza peel) and transfer it to a wire rack to cool for 10 minutes.
  8. Cut with pizza scissors or transfer to a large cutting board and use a pizza cutter and serve.
